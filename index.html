I am a journalist and cookbook author.

My debut cookbook, Made In Taiwan, is a celebration of the island nation I call home. It was a finalist for both the 2024 James Beard Award for Best International Cookbook and the IACP Julia Child First Book Award. My second cookbook, Sitting the Month, will be published by Norton in 2026.

Previously, I was a senior reporter at Goldthread, a video-centric imprint of the South China Morning Post. In the span of two years, I produced more than 140 videos that accumulated a total of 5.8 million views.

Born and raised in Los Angeles, I am currently based in Taipei.
